Work Samples · 03

Data Cleaning Case Study

How a systematic data quality overhaul improved completeness from 61% to 96% ahead of a multi-county programme evaluation.

01
Problem Identification
Audit of 293-record dataset
Routine DQA revealed 39% of records had missing income fields, duplicate Youth IDs, inconsistent county codes, and out-of-range salary values. Root cause: ward-level data entry using paper forms re-entered manually.
02
Tool Redesign
KoboToolbox form with validation rules
Rebuilt data collection forms with mandatory fields, skip logic, and range constraints. Eliminated free-text county entry; replaced with dropdown validation. Added unique ID auto-generation to prevent duplicates.
03
SPSS Cleaning Script
Automated cleaning & flagging routine
Wrote SPSS syntax to flag outliers (salary >3× IQR), identify duplicates, recode inconsistent variables, and generate a data quality report summarising completeness per field per county.
04
Enumerator Retraining
12 community enumerators retrained
Delivered a half-day practical training on KoboToolbox digital entry, data verification protocols, and escalation procedures for anomalies. Distributed quick-reference laminated data quality checklist.
05
Result
96% completeness in one cycle
Next data collection round achieved 96% completeness, eliminating all duplicate IDs and reducing out-of-range values to <1%. Reporting cycle reduced by 2 weeks. Evaluation findings strengthened for donor submission.
